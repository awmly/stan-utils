Clears/Divides

A selection of spacing and dividing elements.

Clears have a base styling of [class^="clear"]{ clear:both; height:0px; } and available in heights of 1, 5, 10, 20, 30, 40 and 50. eg .space-sm{ height:20px; }

Divides have a base styling of [class^="divide"]{ clear:both; border-bottom:solid 1px #ccc; } and available in heights, of 5, 10, 20, 30, 40 and 50. eg .divide20{ height:20px; margin-bottom:20px; }
